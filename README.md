
# E-commerce Delivery Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze delivery performance in an e-commerce platform to identify operational inefficiencies and their impact on customer experience and revenue.

## Dataset
Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset (Olist)

## Key Questions
- How efficient is the delivery process?
- What percentage of orders are delivered late?
- What factors are associated with delivery delays?
- What is the business impact of late deliveries?

## Key Findings

- Approximately 97% of orders are delivered successfully  
- Around 6.6% of orders are delivered late  
- Late deliveries take significantly longer (33.5 days vs 10.5 days)  
- There is a strong relationship between delivery time and delays  
- Late deliveries represent ~7–8% of total revenue (~1M)  

## Business Insights

- Delays are concentrated in a subset of complex orders  
- Longer delivery times are associated with higher delay risk  
- Delivery estimates may not be accurate for long-distance orders  

## Recommendations

- Improve delivery time estimation using historical data  
- Focus on high-risk, long-duration orders  
- Investigate extreme delay cases  
- Optimize logistics for complex deliveries  
- Monitor and reduce non-delivered orders  
- Implement customer experience strategies for delayed orders  

## Tools Used

- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data Analysis
- Business Analysis
